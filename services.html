Business Services

Full-Stack Service Delivery for Global Enterprises

We combine enterprise technology services with mobile management applications to serve customers across Europe and North America with scalable, compliant, and commercially viable digital operations.

Technology Service Lifecycle

Technical service, technical development, technical consultation, technical exchange, technology transfer, and technology promotion for product modernization. We deliver end-to-end support including architecture design, implementation, testing, deployment, and ongoing optimization tailored to your business goals and technical constraints.

Mobile Management Applications

Mobile management applications distributed through Google Play and Apple App Store to support operations management, data collection, reporting, and remote workflows. Features include offline sync, secure authentication, push notifications, background processing, and adaptive UI for diverse device classes.

IT and Integration Services

Information technology consulting services, software development, information system integration services, and data processing services with enterprise-grade monitoring. We architect solutions that connect legacy systems, cloud platforms, and modern data lakes with high-performance, secure integration patterns.

Technical Expertise by Domain

  • Cloud Architecture: AWS, Azure, GCP, and hybrid deployment patterns with multi-region failover and disaster recovery.
  • Backend Development: Node.js, Python, Java, Go microservices with containerization, orchestration, and CI/CD automation.
  • Frontend Engineering: React, Vue, Angular with responsive design, progressive web app (PWA) capabilities, and accessibility compliance.
  • Mobile Platforms: Native iOS/Android, React Native, and Flutter for high-performance mobile experiences.
  • Data and Analytics: ETL pipelines, data warehousing, real-time analytics, business intelligence, and machine learning model deployment.
  • Security and Compliance: Identity management, encryption, secure APIs, audit logging, and regulatory alignment (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, etc.).

Enterprise and Commercial Support

  • Enterprise management consulting and organizational workflow optimization for operational excellence.
  • Marketing planning and strategy implementation for international growth, brand positioning, and market penetration.
  • Business information consulting for digital and offline channels, market research, and competitive intelligence.
  • Sales support for daily necessities, household products, hardware products, electronic products, building materials, decoration materials, and office supplies.
